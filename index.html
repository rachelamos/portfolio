About Me

My fascination with learning began in infancy, which really isn't too unique. All infants are scientists and explorers. But my fascination persisted past this age, and I hope to never lose it. Recently, I took the plunge into an intense coding bootcamp to immerse myself in the languages and study of coding. It's been challenging, but the thrill of unlocking the mysteries of web development has been worth it.

My hope is that from my portfolio, you get a glimpse of what I am able to offer. With my love for learning, I constantly am educating myself on what is new in the industry and what else I can bring to my clients.
